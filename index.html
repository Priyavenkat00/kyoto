Visit Kyoto

Temples

Kyoto is famous for its peaceful temples like Kinkaku-ji and Fushimi Inari Shrine with its endless red torii gates.

Food

Try matcha desserts, Kyoto-style sushi, and delicious street food from Nishiki Market.

Culture Tips

respect traditions, stay quiet at shrines, and try joining a tea ceremony or wearing a kimono in Gion District.
